Lightness is the only color component derived from the CIELAB color model.

Lightness ranges from 0 to 100 where 0 is black and 100 is closer to white, depending on the amount of a (the position between red and green) and b (the position between yellow and blue).

Lightness was the focus of The Pudding's Beauty Brawl article, as it is a concrete color component to assess the inclusivity of foundation shades.
