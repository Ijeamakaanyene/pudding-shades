All of the color compositions are derived from either the HSB color space or CIELAB color model.

HSB stands for hue-saturation-brightness, it is considered a human-friendly way of describing color. CIELAB is based on the human perception of color as well.

Previous methods of describing color relied heavily on RGB, which represents how red, green, or blue a color is.
