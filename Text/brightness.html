Brightness represents, for lack of a better word, the brightness of the color.

Brightness values range from 0 to 100%. Brightness interacts with saturation to represent a color. A color with 0% brightness is black, but a color with 100% brightness is the brightest form of the color. The only exception is if the saturation is at 0%, then the color is just white.

Brightness aligns with how one traditionally think of foundation shades. The foundation shades with the lowest percentage of brightness are also the darkest foundation shades.
